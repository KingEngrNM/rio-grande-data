Rio Grande Data Dashboard

Streamflow and Elephant Butte Reservoir data for the Rio Grande from Lobatos, CO to San Marcial, NM. Data is refreshed automatically each morning from USGS, Colorado DWR, and USBR sources.

Interactive Dashboards

Daily Discharge — Current Water Year

Daily mean discharge for all stations from October 1 through present, including Elephant Butte release and net inflow.

15-Minute Discharge — Past 30 Days

High-resolution instantaneous discharge for the past 30 days, with adjustable y-axis range.

Spreadsheets

RioGrande_WaterYear_Daily.xlsx

Daily mean discharge by station for the current water year, plus Elephant Butte storage, elevation, release, and net inflow.

RioGrande_30Day_15min.xlsx

15-minute instantaneous discharge by station for the past 30 days.

Data sources: USGS National Water Information System, Colorado Division of Water Resources (HydroBase), and U.S. Bureau of Reclamation (RISE). Provisional data subject to revision.
